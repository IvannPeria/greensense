What is StrokeSense?

StrokeSense is an AI handwriting game that teaches Baybayin, an ancient Filipino script. Traditional learning is slow and boring; we make it interactive and engaging using modern technology.

Draw characters digitally, get instant feedback on stroke order and accuracy, and earn rewards to stay motivated. We turn practice into a game, making learning faster and more enjoyable.

Why StrokeSense Matters

Baybayin is fading from cultural memory. StrokeSense helps keep it alive by making the process accessible and fun.

  • Interactive Learning: Write actively, don't just memorize.
  • Instant AI Feedback: Correct mistakes in real-time.
  • Gamified Motivation: Points, badges, and challenges keep engagement high.
  • Preserve Culture: Learn authentic writing and calligraphy styles.

How StrokeSense Works

  1. Handwriting Practice: Draw characters. AI instantly evaluates stroke order, angle, and proportion.
  2. Gamified Levels: Advance through challenges, starting simple and moving to complex ligatures and calligraphy.
  3. Personalized Feedback: AI adapts to your pace, providing extra exercises where you struggle.
  4. Progress Tracking: See visual charts of your accuracy and speed; earn printable certificates.
  5. Cultural Insights: Learn the history and meaning behind each character as you practice.

Top Benefits

  • Learn Faster: AI guidance prevents bad habits.
  • Stay Motivated: Enjoyable gamification elements.
  • Build Confidence: Track progress and celebrate milestones.
  • Preserve Culture: Connect with Filipino heritage digitally.
  • Accessible Anywhere: Web-based on computers, tablets, and touchscreens.

Who Can Use It?
